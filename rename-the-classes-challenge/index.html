Halloween is coming!

Please be advised: Halloween is coming, and for that reason we are going to be shutting down all rooms to create a haunted house. Please refrain from using any of the rooms in this building. If you must use a room, please explore one of the following rooms on the premises:

  • Scullery or butler's pantry on the ground floor
  • Silver polishing room on the second floor
  • Water closets on the third and fifth floors
  • Wine cellar
  • Chocolate room, accessible through the bookcase in the billiard room

If you fail to regard this notice, you may be required to spend the night in the dungeons.
